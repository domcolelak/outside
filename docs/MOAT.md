# Durable product advantage

OUTSIDE's defensible value is accumulated, explainable history rather than presentation alone.

## Foundations present now

- Stable temporal asset identities distinguish new, removed, returned, and changed assets.
- Immutable evidence snapshots preserve raw and normalized observations, provenance, provider reliability, contradictions, and entity-resolution decisions.
- Guardian converts repeated observations into drift, living controls, correlated events, remediation workflow, and delivery history.
- Agency Suite retains cross-customer operational context while keeping source evidence in the customer tenant.
- Provider adapters share bounded, SSRF-safe request and evidence contracts.
- Remediation and finding status creates a longitudinal record of what customers reviewed and resolved.

## Next investments, in order

1. Measure remediation outcomes against later deterministic observations and expose time-to-verified-resolution.
2. Improve temporal entity resolution with explainable alias/ownership links and customer-confirmed corrections.
3. Add opt-in, privacy-reviewed cohort benchmarks using aggregate buckets with minimum cohort size and no reversible tenant labels.
4. Correlate external events with customer-supplied internal incident identifiers without ingesting unnecessary internal telemetry.
5. Add portable evidence exports and migration tooling. Switching cost should come from useful history and workflow, not data captivity.

Do not build cross-customer benchmarks or model training until consent, deletion, regional residency, minimum-cohort privacy, and reproducibility requirements are approved.
